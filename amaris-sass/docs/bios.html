Meet the band

Harvey Dale is the frontman and founder behind Havey Dale and the Cements. Born and raised in Austin, Texas, Harvey started playing the electric guitar at seven years old. In 2018, Harvey decided to bring his journal of lyrics to life and recruited his high school friends, Miles McKenna and Joe Dennis to join his band.

Miles McKenna is the drummer of Harvey Dale and the Cements. Born in Los Angeles, California, Mckenna was inspired by local punk rock bands to take up drumming. After moving to Texas in 2010, he met Harvey Dale and Joe Dennis at a middle school dance. After learning they all shared a passion for playing live music, they started jamming in Dennis' home garage.

Joe Dennis is the bassist and back-up vocals in Harvey Dale and the Cements. The Austinite began playing bass in middle school after Harvey introduced him to the Ramones. He and Harvey are the lyricists behind the the trio's edgy songs.
